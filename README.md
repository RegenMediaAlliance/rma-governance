# Regenerative Media Alliance — Governance

> *The ESPN of regeneration.* An independent, cooperatively owned media support
> system that amplifies the stories enabling a regenerative future.

This repository is the **governance design and change-process** of the Regenerative
Media Alliance (RMA). By ratifying the [Charter](charter.md), members cede the power
to govern from any individual into the written rules of **this repository**. All
changes form part of RMA's public record, made through branches and pull requests.

## Independent, aligned with Quorum 1

RMA is its **own cooperative entity**. It does not subordinate its governance to any
outside organization. It *chooses* to **align structurally with [Quorum 1's
governance model](https://github.com/quorum1/governance)** and to **adopt Q1's Q-Git
change-flow** — because that pattern is proven and keeps the two legible to shared
members — while RMA retains final authority over its own governance. Partnership and
shared tooling, not a chain of command. (See [`docs/entity-and-structure.md`](docs/entity-and-structure.md).)
