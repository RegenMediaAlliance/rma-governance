## Rights (to draft)
- **Participation:** every Contributor-level+ member may participate in governance
  (propose, sponsor, vote by consent).
- **Transparency:** access to relevant metrics, project updates, and governance records
  (the Duty of Transparency, reciprocal).
- **Stewarding rights:** rights to *utilize* collective resources for the mission.
- **Surplus / capital-gains distribution** `[OPEN]` — how surplus is shared, and the
  **qualifying criteria** that determine when/how/to-whom (Josh's note). *"These rights
  help answer not just how we utilize resources, but distribute surplus... setting up
  qualifying criteria so there is no question to when and how a surplus is distributed
  and to whom."*
- **Creator autonomy:** members retain ownership of their own channels/audiences; the
  alliance adds synergy, not control.
- **Exit:** how a member leaves and what they retain.

## Responsibilities (to draft)
- **Quid pro quo / skin in the game:** active contribution (sweat equity, atomic jobs,
  or financial) to participate in the commons.
- **Stewarding responsibilities:** protect collective resources for future members and
  non-human stakeholders.
- **Code of conduct:** `[OPEN]` — safe, inclusive spaces (incl. neurodivergent + BIPOC
  creators).
